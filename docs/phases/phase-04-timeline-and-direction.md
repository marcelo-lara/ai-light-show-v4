# Phase 4: Timeline And Direction

## Goal

Make a show feel directed across the full song. The engine should arrange scenes by musical structure and transition between them intentionally.

## Included Epics

- [Epic 06: Timeline Director](../epics/06-timeline-director.md)
- [Epic 07: Transition System](../epics/07-transition-system.md)

## Deliverables

- Scene timeline format.
- Auto-generated first-pass timeline.
- Manual override hooks.
- Transition types.
- Intensity automation.
- Scene diagnostics.

## Exit Criteria

- A song can contain multiple scene looks.
- Scene changes align to beats, bars, phrases, or sections.
- Transitions can be previewed and validated independently.
