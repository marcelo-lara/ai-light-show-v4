# Phase 2: Musical Analysis IR

## Goal

Turn audio analysis into musical direction signals. Raw frequency bands are useful, but production shows need phrases, drops, energy curves, beat phase, and smoothed modulation sources.

## Included Epics

- [Epic 02: Analysis IR](../epics/02-analysis-ir.md)
- [Epic 05: Modulation System](../epics/05-modulation-system.md)

## Deliverables

- Versioned analysis IR.
- Beat phase and bar phase.
- Smoothed band envelopes.
- Energy and intensity curves.
- Downbeat and phrase candidates.
- Section labels where confidence is high enough.

## Exit Criteria

- Effects can react differently on beat, offbeat, phrase start, breakdown, and drop.
- Modulators are reusable across presets.
- Analysis output includes confidence and diagnostics, not only values.
